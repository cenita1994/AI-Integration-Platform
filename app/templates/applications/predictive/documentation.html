Customer Behavior Prediction Using Artificial Intelligence

This project demonstrates how Predictive Analytics, Machine Learning, and Generative AI can analyze customer information and predict future purchasing decisions.

The system uses customer attributes to classify whether a customer is likely to buy a computer while providing AI-generated explanations.
